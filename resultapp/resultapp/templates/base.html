About ResultsPage

ResultsPage is a fun little app that shows off some of what Soccermetrics software can do. We access data from our web APIs to display results and tables for various types of football competitions all over the world. In future releases, you'll be able to click on the team and check out how their goals and points change over a season.

We hope you enjoy ResultsPage as much as we did creating it.
